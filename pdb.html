Aim & Objectives

Aim

To explore the Protein Data Bank and visualize the three-dimensional structures of biomolecules.

Objectives

  • To study about protein and its structure.
  • To introduce PDB and its importance.
  • To learn how to receive structural data of a protein using PDB database.
  • To describe the PDB file format.

Principle

The Protein Data Bank is a global repository of experimentally determined 3D structures of proteins, nucleic acids, and complexes. Structures obtained using X-ray crystallography, NMR, or cryo-EM are stored in standardized formats. Visualization tools convert atomic coordinate data into graphical models for structural interpretation.
