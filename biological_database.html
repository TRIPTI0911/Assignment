Aim

To familiarize ourselves with biological databases and to retrieve relevant bibliographic, nucleotide, and protein sequence information for biological research.

Objectives

Principle

Biological databases store structured biological information such as DNA, RNA, protein sequences, and literature references. Bibliographic databases provide curated scientific publications, while nucleotide and protein databases store experimentally determined sequences. Efficient database searching enables retrieval of relevant biological data for downstream bioinformatics analyses.
